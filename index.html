My Life as an Athlete

I am a taekwondo athlete and currently ranked #1 in the nation. Taekwondo is the national sport of Korea and is a martial art that focuses on self-defense, discipline, and physical fitness. It combines various kicks, punches, and blocks to develope strength, flexibility, and balence. As a taekwondo athlete on the USA National Team I travel all over the world to various competitions (both domestic and international) and I can say that taekwondo has provided me with new opportunities to meet new people with diverse backgrounds, and experience different perspectives of life, something that is beyond what you can experience in a classroom. One of my proudest moments in my career is winning gold at the 2022 World Championships in Korea. Sometimes my competitions overlap with school, so it is essential for me to maintain a good balence of academics and athletics. I am very dedicated to my training and go up to New Jersey every weekend to train, and sometimes I even spend my winter breaks or spring breaks training. I spent a majority of my summer in Korea training and competing but aside from that it was a great opportunity to experience the culture of Korea and meet new people. Taekwondo keeps me active, and motivated to strive for new goals. I enjoy taekwondo and hope to continue my career to the olympic games in 2028.
